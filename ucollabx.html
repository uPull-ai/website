How uCollabX works

Four stages — and unlike a marketplace listing, none of them are a one-off.

01

🚀 Submit

Any signed-in vendor submits a solution: what it does, who it's for, and supporting evidence for each compliance area.

02

🛡️ Verify

uPull.ai checks five areas — clinical safety, data protection, interoperability, usability & accessibility, and evidence & outcomes.

03

✅ Prove

Status is shown transparently as it's verified — Under review, Provisionally listed, or fully Proven — and re-checked over time, not stamped once and forgotten.

04

🔗 Connect

Every profile links to the vendor's live Community updates and the Academy courses that teach people how to evaluate or deploy that category of solution.

🔐 Rate & review solutions

Same profile as the Academy and Community Hub. Only NHS/public sector logins can post a rating, to keep reviews credible.
