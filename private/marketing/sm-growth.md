# 5. Growth & Social (IG)

## Mission

Build upull.ai's presence on Instagram and other social channels to support brand awareness and top-of-funnel growth, working closely with Marketing but focused specifically on organic social.

## 90-day plan

- Weeks 1–2: Define visual identity and content pillars (product, founder story, AI/startup commentary).

- Weeks 3–6: Post consistently (3–5x/week), start engaging with relevant accounts in the AI/startup space.

- Weeks 7–10: Test Reels/short-form video for reach; identify the 1–2 formats that outperform.

- Weeks 11–13: Build a repeatable content engine and start tracking follower → website → lead conversion.

### Suggested episode arcs (for planning, not required air order)
The 30 episodes group into six narrative arcs of 5 episodes each — useful for batching production and for giving LinkedIn posts a throughline:
1. **Foundations (01–03):** why AI, clinical admin, back-office bottlenecks
2. **Stories (04–06):** pioneers, adopters, common blockers
3. **Playbooks (07–09):** first steps, next steps, governance & safety
4. **Use cases (10–15):** ambient AI, referrals/triage, capacity, rostering, imaging, ward rounds
5. **Scaling & trust (16–21):** community feedback, procurement, EHR integration, change management, ROI, patient trust
6. **Maturity (22–30):** model drift, leadership (ops/clinical), staff wellbeing, scaling sites, failures, agentic workflows, 6-week pilot, closing the loop

### Cadence recommendation
- **Podcast:** one episode every 2 weeks (sustainable for a lean team, and lines up with the existing biweekly meeting rhythm) — roughly a 60-week runway across all 30 episodes if run solo, or compress by batch-recording an arc (5 episodes) per sitting.
- **LinkedIn:** repurpose each episode into 3 posts across its 2-week window, hitting the 3–5x/week target from the Marketing plan without needing separate original content every time:
  - **Teaser (pre-release):** the episode's core question or a provocative stat, no answer given — drives anticipation.
  - **Clip/quote (release day):** pull the single sharpest line or claim from the episode as a native LinkedIn post; link to the full episode.
  - **Takeaway (mid-window):** turn the "Prompt" bullet points into a short carousel or text breakdown — the practical, save-worthy version of the episode.
