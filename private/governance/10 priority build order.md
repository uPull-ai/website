# 10. Priority Build Order

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

For an early-stage supplier, the fastest high-value sequence is:

1. Certificate and corporate records
2. Insurance pack
3. Information security policy
4. Data protection policy and privacy notice
5. Anti-bribery, conflicts, equality, and modern slavery policies
6. NDA, master services agreement, order form, SOW, DPA, and security schedule
7. NHS-specific supplier evidence such as DSP Toolkit planning and any required cyber certification

This order creates a credible baseline for early engagement while building toward deeper NHS and wider public sector readiness.
