# 1. Corporate and Governance Documents

*Part of the uPull.ai Public Sector Supplier Requirement Pack. See `00-overview.md` for the legal review note — this is not legal advice.*

Public buyers routinely conduct supplier due diligence tailored to the requirement, including assessment of supplier capability and assurance evidence.

## Checklist

- [ ] Certificate of incorporation
- [ ] Registered office and company number record
- [ ] Articles of association
- [ ] PSC and shareholder register summary
- [ ] Board or founder decision-making authority matrix
- [ ] List of directors, founders, and key managers
- [ ] Conflicts of interest register and declaration form
- [ ] Subsidiary and group structure note, if applicable
